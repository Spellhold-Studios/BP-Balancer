Version 0.35 (by TotoR)
- Change the way of patching creatures for Drizzt saga and Bonehill in order to keep the initial intention:
    - KEEP will now KEEP every all items but flag them "undroppable" for overpowered one and NPC special items (Drizzt&friends, Fabio)
    - REMOVE will now change item that are dropped by enemies but they will keep initial gears (flagged as undroppable) in order to not change the mod initial intension
- for compatibility with Aurora and IR/IRR and maybe other mods, clck01 changes are no more installed by default and is added in BG1 component
- DEPRECATED out of date components 0,16, related to NTotSC mod and to DSotSC mod
- Remove INSTALL_BY_DEFAULT component and add ALWAY block instead to avoid issues when reinstalling the mod.
- for compatibility It is recommended to install desired component from 17 to 31 of the group "Balancing XP and items of several mods" directly after the last BG1 mods. Components 1 to 15 of the group "Balancing game engine XP" should be installed near the end in the install order.
